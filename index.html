A Story of Family & Resilience

Zaza’s Cleaning Company isn't just a business; it's a legacy. Co-owned by Stasya and her mother, our journey began when Stasya was just 15, helping her mom clean homes after school to support their family.

From those humble beginnings, we've grown into one of Metro Detroit's most trusted cleaning companies. Stasya’s background in healthcare—with a degree in Integrative Studies, nursing experience, and phlebotomy licensure—inspires our mission. We believe in creating clean, healthy environments using safe, non-toxic products.
